How We'll Work Together

My approach ensures you receive personalized care that addresses your unique needs and goals.

1

Initial Consultation

We'll discuss your current concerns, explore your background, and identify your goals for therapy in a comfortable, no-pressure environment.

2

Personalized Plan

Together, we'll develop a customized treatment plan that integrates evidence-based approaches suited to your specific needs and preferences.

3

Ongoing Sessions

Regular sessions provide the space for exploration, skill development, and progress toward your goals in a supportive environment.

4

Progress & Growth

We'll regularly assess your progress, celebrate successes, and make adjustments to ensure your therapy journey remains aligned with your evolving needs.
