My Story

With over 15 years of experience in mental health counseling, I bring warmth, expertise, and a person-centered approach to every therapeutic relationship.

My journey into counseling began with a profound belief in human resilience and the capacity for positive change. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of witnessing the remarkable strength and growth that emerges when individuals are supported with empathy, respect, and evidence-based practices.

I specialize in anxiety, depression, trauma recovery, and relationship issues. My approach integrates cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness practices, creating a holistic framework that addresses both immediate concerns and deeper patterns.

Each client's journey is unique, and I believe in creating a collaborative therapeutic relationship where you feel heard, respected, and empowered to make meaningful changes in your life.

My Philosophy

I believe that therapy is a collaborative journey toward healing and growth. My approach is built on these core principles:

Every Person Is Unique

Your experiences, challenges, and strengths are distinctly yours. Therapy should honor your individuality and be tailored to your specific needs.

Healing Takes Courage

Seeking help and confronting difficult emotions requires tremendous courage. I respect the bravery it takes to begin therapy and navigate the path to healing.

Compassion Is Essential

Sustainable change comes from a place of self-compassion, not self-criticism. I foster an environment where you can develop greater kindness toward yourself.

Balance Evidence & Intuition

Effective therapy balances evidence-based techniques with intuitive understanding. I combine proven methods with a deeply human approach to support your growth.
